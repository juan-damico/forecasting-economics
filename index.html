About

Forecasting Economics

Founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic, Forecasting Economics has grown into one of the leading YouTube channels for applied econometrics and time series analysis — reaching a global community of economists, researchers, and practitioners.

The mission is straightforward: make rigorous applied economics accessible, practical, and replicable — for learners at every level, anywhere in the world.
